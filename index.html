Lighthouse Business Intelligence FAQs

What is Lighthouse Business Intelligence?

Lighthouse Business Intelligence is a brand-neutral platform that brings all of your data into a single view, giving the right people on your team the right information to make decisions in minutes, rather than days.

How does Lighthouse’s approach to BI stand out from the competition?

Business Intelligence centralizes all your commercial data on a single, dynamic platform for quick and easy property performance assessment and decision-making. BI tools like Tableau offer broad solutions, but simply fall short of the nuanced needs of hospitality professionals and commercial teams. They lack our emphasis on real-time, "as-of" data, and the in-depth segmentation we provide. With Lighthouse Business Intelligence, you're not just getting a tool; you're investing in a platform that understands the essence of pace and real-time data, delivering capabilities unmatched by generic BI solutions.

Who is Lighthouse Business Intelligence for?

The whole commercial team. Lighthouse Business Intelligence is built with the needs of Revenue, Marketing, Sales, and Operations teams in mind. The platform also includes roll-up reporting for executive and ownership teams.

How do I understand negotiated business production better?

Lighthouse Business Intelligence allows you to quickly assess negotiated accounts using a variety of criteria. Analyze stay-pattern, booking patterns, displacement and historical production in one simple view. Calculated fields take the guesswork out of both displacement and wish rate analysis!

Do I need Lighthouse Business Intelligence in addition to my RMS?

Lighthouse Business Intelligence is designed to work with your RMS. You can even pull in the RMS forecast to get a more detailed look alongside your OTB reservations and actualized performance. This gives you the visibility required to make detailed forecast adjustments and create a more robust strategy.

How detailed can I get with my strategy?

Go from a quick glance at top performance metrics for the day, week, month, or year to deep strategic dives in just a few clicks. For example, you can analyze how ADR has grown across your entire portfolio over multiple years or see exactly how many room nights you booked from a new promotion on just one night at one hotel.
